FRP Manhole Cover

Product Highlights

  • Lightweight & Durable: Easy to handle without compromising strength.
  • Corrosion Resistant: Resistant to rust, chemicals, and extreme weather conditions.
  • High Load Capacity: Designed to withstand heavy traffic, suitable for various applications.

Description
Upgrade your infrastructure with our FRP Manhole Covers, crafted using advanced fiber-reinforced plastic technology.
These covers are an ideal alternative to traditional cast iron or concrete covers, offering superior durability
and long-lasting performance. Perfect for residential, commercial, and industrial applications, they ensure safety,
reliability, and a modern aesthetic.

Key Features
  • Material: High-grade fiber-reinforced plastic for strength and durability.
  • Weight Significantly lighter than cast iron covers, ensuring easy installation and maintenance.
  • Load Capacity: Available in different load-bearing grades (A15, B125, C250, D400, E600 & F900) to suit various traffic requirements.
  • Corrosion Resistance: Immune to rust, making it perfect for harsh environments.
  • Customization : Available in various sizes, shapes, and colors to fit your needs.
  • Non-Conductive & Non-Slip: Safe for electrical areas and offers enhanced grip for safety.
